They have made remarkable achievements in their respective fields. However, the physiological and psychological sub-healthy state is often overshadowed by the aura of their success. Pay more attention to sub-health, how we live has a direct implication on our health, and in turn, it decides the level of happiness in our lives. Build up healthy mindset and correct negative thinking patterns. Do not search all around for excuses. It's all in your mind, alert yourself when you are making an unhealthy choice. Healthy mindset is an imperative for healthy lifestyle. Be active and start small. Adopt a correct sitting posture, walk, swim, climb etc. If your job requires long hours of sitting, take frequent breaks, get up and stretch. Your activeness should not end only at physical level. Be active on social front, at your workplace, around your neighborhood and wherever possible. Laziness is a slow poison, it junks not only your body but mind as well. It is always said, disease enters by the mouth. Physical and psychological constitutions are also dependent on our food intake. Any compromise on that results in sub-health of the body or restless mind. Eat properly and on time, healthy and delicious food can rejuvenate and rejoice you. Please treasure the health of our nature. Sub-healthy nature always results in sub-healthy human beings. Avoid usage of things that are eco-hazardous and create unhealthy wastage. Preserve our nature and nature will preserve us. Only nature can support a healthy lifestyle.
